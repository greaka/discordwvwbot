WvW Bot

This bot will assign you roles on discord based on your WvW world. For this to work, you will need to provide an API-Key from your account page. The API-Key only needs account permissions and has to include wvwbot in the name.

This bot will save your discord ID and your API-Key. If you want to overwrite your associated API-Key, simply fill out this form again with the new key. If you want to delete everything the bot knows about you, either delete your API-Key on your account page and wait some time or fill out this form with deletemydata.
