WvW Bot

This bot will assign you roles on Discord based on your WvW world. For this to work, you will need to provide an API-Key from your account page. The API-Key only needs account permissions and has to include wvwbot in the name.

This bot will save your Discord ID and your API-Key. If you want to add another API-Key, simply fill out this form again with the new key. If you want to delete everything the bot knows about you, either delete every API-Key that you gave to the bot on your account page and wait some time or fill out this form with deletemydata.

If you just changed your world and want your server roles updated, wait about 6 minutes for the API to update and then fill in this form syncnow. Your roles should get updated soon then.

If you want to invite this bot to your own Discord server, click here. Only give it the permission to manage nicknames if you wish that the usernames will be overwritten with their account names.
